Heading Font: Fjalla One

I will be using Fjalla One as the heading font because it is clean and bold, making the heading clear to see. I also believe Fjalla One compliments the font used in my logo as it is similar, but not exactly the same.

Paragraph Font: Roboto

I chose Roboto for the paragraph font because, just like the heading font, it is clean and modern. I also believe it is easy to read, and pairs well with Fjalla One.
