Purpose

The purpose of this website is to communicate information about community events, news, weather, and more to the citizens of Preston and Soda Springs, as well as surrounding communities. We will accomplish this in two ways. First, we will incorporate important design principles to create a simple, organized, and aesthetically pleasing layout. Page navigation to site content including articles, information, news, and weather forecast will be intuitive and user-friendly. Next, we will include forms on the website that can be submitted to begin correspondance about information and events to be promoted. Not only is the goal to gain website visitors, but also to encourage repeat users to regularly interact with and contribute to the website.
