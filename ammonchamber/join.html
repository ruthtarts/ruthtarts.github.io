Membership Information and Application

Joining the Ammon Chamber of Commerce is a rewarding way to uplevel your business and professional life. Membership offers numerous benefits, giving you exclusive access to resources not found anywhere else. These benefits include, but are not limited to:

  • Organized events for networking and sharing information with other local professionals
  • Professional development and discussions led by master presenters
  • Exclusive member-to-member referral program
  • Inclusion in the member directory
  • Credibility among other professionals and the community
  • Advocacy and potential to influence your local leaders on issues that impact your business
