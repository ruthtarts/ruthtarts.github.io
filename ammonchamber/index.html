What is the purpose of the chamber of commerce?


A chamber of commerce is a voluntary partnership of businesses and professionals working together to build a healthy economy and improve the quality of life in a community. (source)

The goals of the Ammon Chamber of Commerce include promotion of local businesses, implementation of educational opportunities for workforce development,
and coordination with elected officials to create activities that benefit the professional community.

To contact the board of directors, please email board@ammonchamber.com or visit the contact page.
